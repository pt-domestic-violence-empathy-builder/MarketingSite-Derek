Financial insecurity is the No. 1 reason survivors of domestic violence remain in or return to abuse. In order to break the cycle of violence, we must remove obstacles to financial security for survivors. This interactive calculator will help those not experiencing violence to understand how much it would cost for them to leave their lives.

Click to Get Started

The calculator includes a personal monthly budget to determine what costs you would need to cover, and a way to calculate the costs of relocating.The monthly personal budget allows you to enter any ongoing costs you might have while trying to relocate. After entering all relevant fields, a page will combine your total monthly expenses plus the cost of relocation.

If this calculator leads you to give back, we would love to partner with you in helping provide safety and financial stability for survivors of domestic violence. You can donate at our website.

If you or someone you know is experiencing domestic violence, there are anonymous advocacy groups that can help. You can contact the National Domestic Violence Hotline at www.thehotline.org or call at 1-800-799-SAFE (7233).
